UpLevo

October 2018 - February 2019
UpLevo is a field study service aiming at connecting brands with their actual customers. We started with collecting target customers' feedback about tasting, packaging etc for food startups, then provided consulting service and continuing field study.
As the founding designer, I worked with sensory analysts, food startups and target users to come up with apps for users, a web-based portal for food companies with analytics and suggestions from sensory analysts.
