DareMe

Feb 2019 - present
DareMe is a social accountability app facing both consumers and enterprises. DareMe has served corporations, education organizations and non-profit organizations with a DAU of 400 paid users.
As the sole designer, I led a complete redesign for a social activity iOS/Android apps, including core flows like community exploration, event participation, daily check-in and instant messaging. The new design contributed to the growth of MAU from 500 to 1.7k with $20k in revenue after launch in 2 months. The app is currently at 4.8 stars.
